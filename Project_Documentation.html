2. Problem Statement & Strategic Inference

The Core Problem

Banks today face a "Blind Spot" in credit risk. Standard models rely heavily on credit formulation (Bureau scores, Income), which are lagging indicators. They fail to capture:

  • Velocity Risks: Rapid depletion of savings or sudden spikes in gambling.
  • Behavioral Drift: Change in app login patterns indicating financial anxiety.
  • Nuanced Default: The difference between a customer who cannot pay (job loss) and one who will not pay (strategic default).

What Barclays Should Infer

Empathy

Shift from "Collections" to "Assistance", building long-term loyalty.

Proactivity

Act 60 days before the first missed EMI, saving capital.

Precision

Treat every customer uniquely based on "Ability vs Willingness".

5. ML Model Approach

We utilize an **Ensemble of Heuristic & Statistical Models** rather than a single black-box Deep Learning model. This ensures **Explainability (XAI)**, which is critical for regulatory compliance.

The 3-Agent System

  • Financial Agent (Weight 50%): Traditional metrics (DTI, Utilization). Base Accuracy: 85% on historic data.
  • Behavioral Agent (Weight 25%): Psychometric proxies (Gambling, Night Logins). Increases detection of "Strategic Defaulters" by 40%.
  • Velocity Agent (Weight 25%): Rate of change (Spending spikes, Savings depletion). Captures "Sudden Distress" signals.
